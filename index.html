Etch-A-Sketch

Instructions

  • Click and hold on the grid to start drawing.
  • Right click and hold to delete colored tiles.
  • Middle click to grab the color of the highlighted tile.
  • Select options from the right menu to customize your Etch-A-Sketch.
  • Change grid size with the slider below the grid.
    NOTE: Resizing the grid WILL erase your drawing!
